Introducing Our New Educator Portal

This new tool will allow educators to create BizWorld.org accounts to manage classes, upload student rosters, administer pre- and post- assesments, and best of all - see student results instantly!

Register Your Class

Make it official by registering your current classes, as well as past and future ones. Keep track of all your activities, get support from our staff when you need it, and be the first to hear about exciting campaigns for our educators.

Administer Assessments

Measure your students’ learning with our pre- and post-assessments. Upload your student roster or add your students individually to the class. Then, simply share the link with your students to have them complete the assessments online.

See Student Results

Once your class completes the assessments, get instant feedback on how your students did. Use "pre" data to help inform your instruction.  Use "post" data to see how much your students learned about business and entrepreneurship!
