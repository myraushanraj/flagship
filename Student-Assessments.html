Student Assessments

Each of BizWorld.org's entrepreneurship programs has a built-in assessment to accurately measure what your students learned from the program. We've updated the old assessment links to direct teachers to our new Educator Portal where they can log into their accounts, administer pre- and post- assessments, and see students results instantly!

BizWorld.org uses the information from students' assessments to explain the impact of our programs on students around the world. We appreciate you taking the time to have your students complete them. This valuable data helps us spread our message and keep our programs available at a low cost to educators like you.
